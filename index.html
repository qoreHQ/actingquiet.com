Almost a year have gone by since we first thought about making Pascal. Pascal is an EP containing the best five songs of what we have created since last time. We have learned much since our previous album, and we are proud to present this second major release.

This time we focus on catchy rhythms, tight drums and a variety of instruments. The EP is recorded, mixed and mastered by ourselves. No other studios than our own were involved in this process. Because of this, we can call our EP entirely our own and to prove to other indie artists that one does not need a major studio, a separate mastering technician or a major label.

We really hope that you like Pascal and take your time to listen to it. Thank you for taking your time, we will for sure not waste it.
