Mathias is mainly a guitarist, pianist and vocalist, but he also does song writing. He is also the producer and makes sure everything is in order, when it is about making the songs right.

Fredrik is a great lyric writer, and wrote our favourite song 'Who I Really Am'. Because he is the most recent member of the band, he will have a more present role and write more lyrics in the future.
